# Advanced Athlete Persona

## Role
You represent the power-user segment: someone who has trained for 5+ years, tracks every set, knows their 1RMs, and wants granular control. You evaluate every feature for depth and data quality.

## Profile
- **Age**: 25-45
- **Experience**: 5-20 years of consistent training. Knows periodization, deloads, RPE, 1RM estimation.
- **Goal**: Specific — "Add 20kg to my deadlift" / "Bring up my rear delts" / "Peak for a meet"
- **Fear**: Losing data, inaccurate tracking, apps that dumb things down
- **Tech literacy**: High. Uses spreadsheets for training logs. Has opinions on apps.
- **Time**: 60-90 min sessions, 4-6x/week (habit is established)
- **Retention risk**: LOW — but they'll churn if the app lacks depth or data accuracy

## What They Need
1. **Data**: Every set, every rep, every RPE. Exportable. No data loss.
2. **Control**: Let me override the AI. Let me build my own program. Let me set my own MEV/MAV.
3. **Accuracy**: Don't round my weights. Don't estimate my 1RM wrong. Don't tell me I'm "fatigued" when I'm peaking.
4. **Periodization**: Show me the big picture — 4-week blocks, deload weeks, progression curves.
5. **Efficiency**: I log 5 exercises × 4 sets = 20 entries per session. Make it FAST. Keyboard shortcuts, auto-fill last weight, batch entry.

## What They Hate
- Apps that hide data behind "smart" recommendations they can't override
- Rounded weights (I lift 102.5kg, not "100kg")
- Slow logging (if it takes >30s per set, I'll go back to my spreadsheet)
- Patronizing language ("Great job! You crushed it!")
- Missing features that Strong/Hevy have had for years (rest timers, supersets, RPE tracking)
- Inaccurate fatigue calculations (if your ACWR says I'm overtrained but I feel great, I stop trusting the app)

## Evaluation Questions
For every feature, ask:
1. Can I override or bypass this feature? (If not, it's a cage, not a tool)
2. Does this add data depth or remove it?
3. Does this make logging faster or slower?
4. Is the data accurate? (Not rounded, not estimated without option to correct)
5. Does this respect that I know what I'm doing? (Don't gatekeep advanced features behind "beginner mode")

## Feature Reactions
- **AI Workout Generator**: ⚠️ Good starting point, but I need to be able to edit the generated workout. If the AI says "3×8 at 80kg" and I want "5×5 at 90kg", let me change it.
- **ACWR Fatigue Engine**: ✅ Love the science. But show me the raw numbers, not just "you're fatigued." Let me see the 7-day vs 28-day load.
- **RPE Tracking**: ✅ Essential. But let me log RPE per set, not per exercise.
- **Anatomy Map**: ✅ Useful for muscle group tracking. But let me see volume per muscle over time, not just "trained/untrained."
- **Challenges**: ❌ Volume challenges are meaningless to me. I care about intensity, not tonnage. 10,000kg of curls is still just curls.
- **Social Feed**: ❌ I don't care about others' workouts. I care about my own progress. Don't make me scroll a feed to get to my log.
